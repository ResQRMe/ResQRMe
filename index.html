🔒 Data Privacy Notice

Republic Act No. 10173 — Data Privacy Act of 2012

Purpose of Data Collection

RESQ ME collects and processes your personal and medical information solely to generate your emergency health profile and QR identification tag. Your data enables first responders to access critical health information during emergencies.

What We Collect

  • Personal information (name, date of birth, sex, address, contact details)
  • Emergency contact information
  • Medical information (allergies, medications, chronic conditions, medical history)
  • Health insurance and primary care provider details
  • Patient photo (optional)

How Your Data is Used

  • Stored securely in a database linked to your unique Patient ID
  • Accessible via your personal QR code to authorized viewers (e.g., first responders)
  • Never sold, shared, or disclosed to third parties without your consent
  • Used only for emergency identification and health profile purposes

Your Rights Under RA 10173

  • Right to be informed — you are notified before data is collected
  • Right to access — you may request a copy of your stored data
  • Right to rectification — you may correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — you may request deletion of your record
  • Right to data portability — you may request a copy in usable format

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your RESQ ME profile is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us at support@resqme.com.

Legal Basis: This notice is issued in compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173) and its Implementing Rules and Regulations, administered by the National Privacy Commission (NPC) of the Philippines.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is RESQ ME and how does it work?

RESQ ME generates a unique QR code linked to your emergency health profile. First responders scan your QR code to instantly access your critical health data — allergies, medications, blood type, and emergency contacts — without needing a password.

Is my personal data safe?

Yes. Your data is stored securely and is never sold or shared with third parties. It is only accessible via your personal QR code, and your rights under the Philippine Data Privacy Act (RA 10173) are fully respected.

What if I need to update my medical information?

Contact us at support@resqme.com with your Patient ID and we will help you update your profile. A self-service update feature is coming soon.

How long does delivery take for physical products?

Delivery typically takes 3–7 business days within Metro Manila, and 5–10 business days for provincial orders, depending on your location and courier availability.

Can I get a refund or replacement?

Yes. If your product arrives damaged or defective, contact us within 7 days of receipt for a replacement. Refunds are handled case-by-case.

About

About RESQ ME

RESQ ME is a student-led health technology startup that provides emergency medical identification solutions through digital and physical products. The company utilizes QR codes and NFC technology to grant first responders instant access to a user's critical health data, such as allergies and chronic conditions. Their offerings include a free digital profile, as well as affordable keychains and card-style tags designed for quick scanning during crises. Operated by a specialized student management team, the venture prioritizes a lean, flat organizational structure to maintain operational flexibility and data privacy. By streamlining the intake of vital information, the service aims to improve emergency response times and personal safety for all users.
